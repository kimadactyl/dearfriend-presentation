Dear Friend

A letter-writing project celebrating women in public life and struggles for liberation.

@dearfriendmcr

Correcting the balance of history

  • Women under-represented in history — both as researchers and subjects
  • Women's work not seen as work — women's activism seen as less important than mens'
  • Chronic lack of information about women who made a difference

Designing Dear Friend

  • Want to unearth new stories — tomorrow's famous women
  • Let people tell us who is important to them
  • Make it easy to participate — not require endless research
  • Self-archiving project — something that creates permanent artifacts

How it works

  • Write a letter to a woman who had an impact on public life
  • We scan and transcribe your letter and add it to the site
  • Also run various workshops — next one 2nd March at PHM
  • Exhibition currently on at Nexus Art Cafe — last few days
